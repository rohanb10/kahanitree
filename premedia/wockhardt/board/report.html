The consolidated total revenue of the Company for the financial year ended 31st March, 2019 stood at ` 4,179 crore as compared to ` 4,057 crore of previous year. Earnings before interest, tax, depreciation and amortization (EBITDA) during the year showed marked improvement from earlier ` (55) crore in FY 2017-18 to `135 crore in FY 2018-19. The total Comprehensive loss for the year stood at ` 208 crore vis-à-vis total Comprehensive loss of ` 528 crore of previous year.

On Standalone basis, the Company registered total revenue of ` 2,181 crore as compared to ` 2,534 crore during previous year. Total Comprehensive Income for the year stood at ` (83) crore vis-à-vis ` 69 crore of previous year.

Performance of the Company has also been discussed in detail in the ‘Management Discussion and Analysis Report’ forming part of this Annual Report.
