Veeva

Veeva is a global leader in the supply of cloud based software for the global life sciences industry. We are proud to be certified as a Veeva Level __ Multichannel Content Partner – the highest level of accreditation – making us one of a few select agencies around the world that provide the best in class service across the Veeva ecosystem.

We are certified to develop tablet materials for Veeva CRM, design and develop Rep triggered emails and develop material used in remote detailing.

IQVIA OCE

OCE is an innovative strategy that enables Life Sciences companies to integrate customer-engagement activities across the organization, powered by strong information management.

We are certified to to navigate IQVIA’s revolutionary Orchestrated Customer Engagement Platform as well as to create platform-compliant content.

We have created OCE visual aids (e-Detailers) for several markets across Europe, Asia Pacific and South Africa.

IQVIA eContent

MI Touch is a platform, similar to Veeva, allowing for delivery via an iPad of HTML based slides from a cloud-based technology solution.

We are certified to develop material for MiTouch and have created iDetailers across European markets in the pharmaceutical industry.
